🧼 Biosecurity Guidelines

  • Wash hands before and after animal contact.
  • Disinfect equipment after each use.
  • Use proper PPE in restricted areas.
  • Quarantine new or sick animals immediately.
  • Keep detailed records of treatments and movement.
